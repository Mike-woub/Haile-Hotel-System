About Haile Hotel Wolaita

Welcome to Haile Hotel Wolaita, where modern luxury meets traditional Ethiopian hospitality. Nestled in the heart of Wolaita Sodo, our hotel is designed to offer a comfortable and memorable experience for every guest. We provide beautifully appointed rooms equipped with modern amenities, ensuring a restful stay. Our dedicated staff is committed to delivering exceptional service, making you feel right at home. Enjoy a culinary journey at our on-site restaurant, where our chefs prepare a delightful array of international and local dishes, crafted to satisfy every palate.

At Haile Hotel Wolaita, we believe in creating an environment that fosters relaxation and enjoyment. Guests can unwind at our spa, stay active in our fitness center, or take a refreshing dip in the pool. Whether you are here for business or leisure, we offer versatile conference facilities to cater to all your needs. Discover the culture and beauty of Wolaita with us, and let us make your stay an unforgettable one.
