About Haile

Haile Gebresilassie is a renowned Ethiopian long-distance runner and the founder of Haile Hotel and Resorts. An Olympic champion and world record holder, he has inspired countless athletes with his dedication and perseverance. Beyond athletics, Haile has transformed the hospitality industry in Ethiopia, creating luxurious accommodations that reflect the country's rich culture and heritage. His commitment to excellence and community development is evident in every aspect of his ventures, making him a celebrated figure both in sports and business.
